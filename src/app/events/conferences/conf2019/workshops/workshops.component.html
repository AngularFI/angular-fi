This immersive instructor-led workshop covers advanced concepts and guides you towards building highly-performant data-centric business applications in Angular

    • Route Transitions (Animations module)

    • Performance for Data Binding with OnPush

    • Ahead of Time (AOT) Compilation, Tree Shaking and bundle analyzer

    • Improve ngFor with trackBy
    • Caching with Service Worker
