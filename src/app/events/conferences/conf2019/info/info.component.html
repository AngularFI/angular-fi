Time, place, tickets, travel, accommodation, special needs and more

How do I get a ticket?
Register in our meetup group page. You'll need to join the meetup network, if you're not already a member. Membership is free.

How much do tickets cost?
If you have a training/conference budget, your ticket price is €95.
Is there an early-bird rate?
Yes. Act fast. The early bird price saves you 25% and it ends soon!
Can I get a discount or a free entry?
Please do apply for a free ticket if you have no budget available for training or career development. Free or reduced-price entry may be available if you are
  • a student
  • considering or starting a web-dev career, not yet employed
  • volunteering to be part of the conference team
  • a contributor to an Angular Finland project on Github
To qualify, you'll need to show your student status or describe the path you are on to enter this field, giving reasons why paying for a ticket is not possible for you at this time. Contact the event organizing team listed on this page.
When will the program and speakers be published?
January 2019.
How do I register for a workshop?
Buy a ticket to one of the workshops listed in the meetup group. Workshops are not included in the main ticket €95 price.
How can I get involved?
Contact any Angular Finland organizer to find out about volunteering. Do you have any of these skills: video production, photography, event management, project management, customer service mentality, great personal organisation, social media? We'd love to hear from you.
What are the benefits of sponsorship?
Visibility in and around the event; guaranteed tickets for your team; space for a stand; opportunities to get introduced to the full audience.
