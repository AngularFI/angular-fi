About Angular Finland

We're an association formed to bring Angular people together in Finland. We're here to help people enter the industry, to develop their skills and to provide a platform for experts to share their knowledge with the Angular community.

Our main activities are meetups, larger events in Finland and contributing to the ngVikings conference.

We welcome new members at any level. You can join for free at our meetup.com page.
