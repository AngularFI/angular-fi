Take your place in the Angular community

You have many options for getting involved or you can help us find new ways to connect and collaborate

Join
What? Not a member yet? Join immediately.

Membership is free. You just need to stay positive and you'll always be welcome, whatever your Angular skill level.

Join
Come to a meetup
Learn something cool, eat pizza and make a friend

Yes, it's OK to nerd out about the Angular apps you and your peers are working on. Be part of the discussion or just come to listen.

Register for the next one
Give a talk
Share something Angular with Finland's most supportive audience

Celebrate a successful project, share something you've discovered, tell us what you're working on or thinking about. And if you dare to do some live-coding, this is the stage for you!

You are welcome to present any topic with an Angular angle. Shorter and longer presentations possible. If you want help preparing, the team is here for you.

Propose a talk
Host an event
Our Angular meetups are hosted by Finland's finest companies

Take this opportunity to show that you're serious about supporting the developer community. Hosting an event is easy, inexpensive and rewarding for you and your guests.

You just need a meeting space for 20-40 people, some refreshments and to help people find their way to the right spot in your building. Usually in the Helsinki area, other large cities are also possible.

Offer your space
Sponsor
Take your visibility to a new level and feel the love of the Angular crowd

Angular Finland and ngVikings offer sponsorship opportunities. We're not-for-profit volunteer organisation, so 100% of your financial generosity goes directly into Angular events or other benefits such as scholarships.

Visibility options include hosting or branding at meetups, larger event sponsorship with an expo stand, appearing in this website as a prominent partner, being included in social media content and newsletters.

For a small financial investment you'll connect with talented Angular developers as an insider at our events. You'll also link your brand in a strongly positive way with a grassroots developer community. Check ngVikings sponsorship to be part of the world's best-loved Angular conf. Or keep it local and support the Angular community here in Finland.

Be an event sponsor
Code
Use your Angular super-powers to make something for the community

The Angular Finland website needs love and web dev talent to grow into a rich resource for Angular developers in Finland.

If you have expertise and a little time to spare, tackle an issue on our Github or come up with new content or a new feature. Get experience as an opensource contributor and build your skills. Create issues, make pull requests, comment and collaborate. Make a difference without even going outside!

Contribute to our opensource
Volunteer
Are you a great organiser? Are you a creative? Are you the friendly type? Are you all of these?

If you are great with people, great with a camera, great with a sketchpad or have other event management skills there is a role for you at our Big Hangout. Volunteer Angular enthusiasts will welcome participants at events, to help guest speakers navigate and create a brilliant mini-conf experience.

If you've got organisational and event management skills, photography skills, videography skills, design skills we need you to be part of the team for our Big Hangout.

Join the event team
Organize
Are you ready to make your love affair with Angular a public, long-term thing?

Join the team of organizers making things happen for Angular Finland members.

Whether you can offer a thick or tiny thin slice of your week, you just need to be ready to take responsibility for an area of Angular Finland activity that you feel passionate about, or where your skills can have the most impact.

As an organizer, you have freedom to make an existing activity better, or to take Angular Finland into new areas.

Be part of the team
Mentor
Will you help a newer Angular developer onto the ladder to success?

Mentoring is a rewarding experience for all sides. Bring your skills as a coach and as an experienced developer to help others ride the Angular wave.

Start mentoring
Communicate
Do you like to break the silence? Do you believe in documenting your best work?

There's so much going on in the Angular Finland community that we never get to hear about. You can be the person who writes the tweets, creates the blog posts, does interviews and shares news about the amazing work of Angular developers in Finland.

Make your voice heard
